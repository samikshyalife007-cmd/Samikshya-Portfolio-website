I'm a Software QA Engineer with a BSc in Computing (Upper Second Class Honours, 66.89%) from London Metropolitan University, currently working at Maitri Holding Services in Kathmandu where I focus on healthcare data quality, HIPAA compliance, and ELT pipeline testing.

My background spans fintech at F1Soft Technologies, e-learning QA, and automation engineering — giving me a broad perspective on how quality looks across different domains.

My research interest lies at the intersection of cognitive biases and quality assurance — how human decision-making affects the bugs we find (and miss). This is a keen area of interest I hope to explore further — how we design better tools and processes for testers and users alike.

Outside of tech, I was a Miss Nepal 2024 finalist, Nepal representative for Miss Teen International 2022, and serve as Vice President of the KMC Alumni Association.
